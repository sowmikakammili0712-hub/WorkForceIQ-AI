# KPI Dictionary

## Overview

This document defines the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) used throughout the WorkForceIQ AI solution.

Each KPI includes its business purpose, calculation logic, interpretation, and the dashboards where it is used. This ensures consistent reporting and governance across the analytics platform.

# KPI Definitions

## Total Tickets

**Description**

Represents the total number of support tickets handled during the selected reporting period.

**Business Value**

Provides an overall view of operational workload and demand.

---

## SLA Compliance

**Description**

Percentage of tickets resolved within the agreed Service Level Agreement (SLA).

**Business Goal**

≥ 85%

**Business Impact**

Lower SLA compliance may indicate understaffing, process bottlenecks, or operational inefficiencies.

---

## Average Resolution Time

**Description**

Average time taken to resolve customer support tickets.

**Business Goal**

Lower values indicate improved operational efficiency.

---

## Escalation Rate

**Description**

Percentage of tickets escalated to higher support levels.

**Business Goal**

≤ 30%

**Business Impact**

High escalation rates may indicate knowledge gaps, complex issues, or resource constraints.

---

## Agent Utilization

**Description**

Measures the proportion of productive time spent handling customer requests.

**Business Goal**

Maintain optimal utilization without overloading agents.

---

## Backlog

**Description**

Number of unresolved or pending customer tickets.

**Business Goal**

Minimize backlog while maintaining SLA performance.

---

## Forecast Capacity

**Description**

Measures whether available staffing can support forecasted ticket demand.

**Business Goal**

≥ 90%

**Business Impact**

Lower capacity indicates potential staffing shortages and increased SLA risk.

---

## Executive Health Score

**Description**

Composite operational KPI summarizing overall business performance.

**Business Purpose**

Provides executives with a single, high-level indicator of operational health.

---

## AI Governance Health Score

**Description**

Measures the effectiveness of governance controls applied to AI-assisted recommendations.

**Business Purpose**

Supports transparency, explainability, and responsible AI practices.

---

## Decision Trust Index

**Description**

Indicates the confidence level associated with AI-generated recommendations.

**Business Purpose**

Helps decision-makers evaluate whether recommendations are suitable for operational use.
