# User Stories

## Story 1

As an Operations Manager,

I want weekly ticket forecasts,

so that I can plan staffing.

---

## Story 2

As a Workforce Planner,

I want occupancy calculations,

so that I can optimize workforce utilization.

---

## Story 3

As a CEO,

I want AI-generated executive summaries,

so that I can understand business performance within 30 seconds.
